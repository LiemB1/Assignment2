GAME INSTRUCTIONS:
You will need to start the game, and it will ask you which mode you would like to enter (30 seconds) or (60 seconds). Enter 30 or 60 to start the game according to the duration you typed. Litters will start spawning from the right, your job is to collect this litters either by pressing the "COLLECT" button or pressing the "Space" key on your keyboard. You can move your character by pressing the "left", "right", "up", "down" key. Enjoy the game!
